Cricketing Style and Achievements :

Rohit Sharma's batting style is characterized by exquisite timing, effortless stroke play, and the ability to dominate bowlers across all formats of the game. He is a master of the limited-overs formats, with an impressive record of multiple double centuries in ODI and being the only player to achieve three double centuries in the history of ODI cricket. In addition to his batting prowess, Rohit's leadership skills have shone brightly as well. He has led the Indian cricket team to numerous victories as captain.
